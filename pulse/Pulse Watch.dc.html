Method & model card
How it works, what it catches, and its limits.
Pulse Watch learns each person's own calm baseline on-device and flags physiological deviations — primarily the body's stress response — for a human to review. It is sensitivity-first and will raise some false alarms by design. It is an early-warning aid, not a diagnostic device, and does not detect medical conditions. Everything runs locally; raw signals stay on the device.
Method & metrics
Detects deviations from a person's own calm baseline. The contribution is the method & on-device deployment — not beating a smartwatch on raw accuracy.
